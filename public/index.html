What I Do

I am a 2D animator and graphic design professional with more than 10 years experience in animation and computer games industry in USA and Europe. As an art director at Secretbuilders.com I led a project for Oxford University Press, developing more than 200 Hidden Object games out of classic books. I was responsible for UI design, background and characters development, liaising with partners, designers, account executives and website developers. My work as part of a broader production team in Magic World studio was awarded in 2000 with an Emmy. I am accustomed to working with technical software packages such as Flash, Mays, LightWave Softimage and Cinema 2D building accurate, detailed frame by frame visuals.
